FAQ
Common Questions
What are the plan tiers? ▾
Copper ($1,500/mo): 4 HD videos, 1x3hr shoot, FB ads. Gold ($3,000/mo): 8 videos, 4x2hr shoots, scriptwriting, camera coaching, FB ads. Platinum ($5,000/mo): 20 videos, 8 shoots/mo, photos, Google + FB ads, strategy calls, full social management.
How do I switch between roles? ▾
Click the role button at the bottom of the sidebar (or open the hamburger menu on mobile). Each role has its own navigation, dashboard, and messages.
How does content approval work? ▾
Content moves through the pipeline: Scripting → Editing → Review → Approved → Scheduled → Published. When it reaches "Review", the client sees it with the full script. They can Approve (with confetti!) or Request Changes with notes.
What integrations are supported? ▾
QuickBooks (expenses), Zoom (calls), Google Ads API, Meta Ads Manager, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok. Stripe and Twilio available to connect.
Does it work on mobile? ▾
Yes. The sidebar collapses into a hamburger menu. All modals are centered and responsive. Role switcher accessible from the mobile sidebar.
Can the AI really write scripts? ▾
In demo mode, the AI generates realistic scripts with HOOK/SETUP/BODY/CTA format plus shoot notes. With a backend, this connects to Claude AI for truly personalized scripts based on the client's brand kit, industry, and audience.
