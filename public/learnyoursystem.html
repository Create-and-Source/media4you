Role-Based Access
5 Roles, One Platform
Each team member sees exactly what they need. Switch roles in the sidebar to explore.
A
Admin / Owner
Full access to everything. Run the agency.
01
Dashboard — Your Command Center
See MRR, active clients, content performance, and quick actions at a glance.
The dashboard shows a welcome hero with your greeting and total MRR. Below that, 4 KPI cards (clients, revenue, videos, scheduled posts) with hover animations. The right column shows content performance by client and a quick actions grid. Everything is clickable — KPIs link to their respective pages.
02
Pipeline — Drag-and-Drop Content Tracking
All scripts and videos across all clients in one Kanban board.
6 columns: Scripting → Editing → Review → Approved → Scheduled → Published. Drag any card between columns to change its status. Click a card to see details, change status via dropdown, or leave comments. Filter by client at the top. Overdue items show in red. High-priority items have a red bar.
03
Content Library — All Your Assets
Browse finished videos, raw footage, and photos. Download, share, or reuse.
Filter by type (Video, Raw Footage, Photos). Each asset shows format, file size, duration, and publishing status. Click to expand and see full specs. Download files, copy share links, or add to the content calendar for reuse.
04
Post Scheduler — Multi-Platform Publishing
Schedule posts across Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook with peak time optimization.
See all upcoming posts with color-coded platform pills. Peak time posts are highlighted. Click any post to publish immediately, reschedule, or preview. Stats at the top show scheduled count, drafts, and this week's posts.
05
Clients — Deep Detail on Every Account
6-tab client detail: Overview, Content, Ads, Shoots, Analytics, Brand Kit.
Click any client to see their full profile. Overview shows plan deliverables, connected accounts (IG, FB, TikTok, Google), team assignments, and activity timeline. Content tab shows their pipeline with clickable items. Analytics shows follower growth, engagement, and ROI. Brand Kit shows colors, tone, hashtags, and audience.
06
Revenue & Expenses — Agency Financials
Track MRR, profit/loss, expenses, and plan distribution.
Revenue page shows total revenue, MRR by client (expandable), profit margin, and ARR. Plan distribution shows Copper/Gold/Platinum client counts with revenue per tier. Expenses page has QuickBooks sync, add/edit/delete expenses, recurring vs one-time breakdown. Click any expense amount to edit inline.
07
Reports — Client Performance Reports
Generate monthly reports with one click. Download PDF or email to clients.
Select a client and generate their monthly report. Shows videos delivered, followers gained, avg views, engagement rate, ad spend, conversions, content delivered checklist, top performing content, platform breakdown, and ROI summary. Download as PDF, share link, or email directly to the client.
08
Settings — Branding & Integrations
Connect accounts, manage integrations, customize branding, toggle dark mode.
Connected accounts: Instagram, Facebook, Meta Ads, TikTok. Integrations: Zoom, QuickBooks, Stripe, Twilio, Google Ads API. Branding: edit business name, tagline, primary color, upload logo, client portal URL. Appearance: dark mode toggle.

FAQ
Common Questions
What are the plan tiers? ▾
Copper ($1,500/mo): 4 HD videos, 1x3hr shoot, FB ads, social management. Gold ($3,000/mo): 8 videos, 4x2hr shoots, scriptwriting, camera coaching, FB ads. Platinum ($5,000/mo): 20 videos, 8 shoots/mo, photos, Google + FB ads, strategy calls, full social management.
How do I switch between roles? ▾
Click the role button at the bottom of the sidebar (or open the hamburger menu on mobile). Select any role to see the platform from that perspective. Each role has its own navigation, dashboard, and messages.
How does the content approval workflow work? ▾
Content moves through the pipeline: Scripting → Editing → Review → Approved → Scheduled → Published. When content reaches "Review", the client sees it in their portal with the full script. They can Approve (sends it to production) or Request Changes (with notes explaining what to fix). All decisions are logged in the approval history.
What integrations are supported? ▾
QuickBooks (for expenses), Zoom (for calls), Google Ads API, Meta Ads Manager, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok. Stripe and Twilio are available to connect. All managed from Settings.
Can I use this on mobile? ▾
Yes. The sidebar collapses into a hamburger menu on mobile. All modals and sheets are centered and responsive. The role switcher is accessible from the mobile sidebar.
